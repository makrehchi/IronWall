Expected Value Analysis
EV = C × (3W − 2)  where W = win rate, C = credit collected · Stop loss = 2× credit means: win → +C, lose → −2C  ·  Break-even: W > 66.7%  ·  Cells below break-even shown in red

Positive Expectancy
Positive Expectancy (PE) = EV / Max Loss = (3W − 2) / 2  ·  Measures edge as a fraction of capital risked per trade  ·  PE > 0 = profitable system  ·  PE = 0.10 means you earn $0.10 for every $1.00 at risk
