Wing size dominates
Expectancy rises consistently as wing widens. Wings ≤20 are high-risk at any credit ≥$2. Wings ≥30 are positive across all credit levels.

Credit matters at small wings
Wing 10 swings from +0.01 at $1 credit to −0.42 at $3 credit. At wing 35+, raising credit improves expectancy.

Calls slightly weaker than puts
At small wings and high credits, calls underperform puts. Both converge near +0.25 at wing 50–55 / $3 credit.

Sweet spot
